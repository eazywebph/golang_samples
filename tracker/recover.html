About

This is a test project that runs on Golang that I intent to use personally to track my crypto investments. I don't have a significant amount of money on the crypto, not that I don't want to but it's what I can only afford. I couldn't call my wallet, a "wallet" due to the miniscule amount of my investments. Hence, the purse. Maybe someday, it'll grow and I'll no longer call my wallet a purse but this website, I hope it will live on by then.

I'm sharing this in the open in hopes of some people would like to have a different approach in keeping track of their investments. Sure, it doesn't have the advance functionalities that the other portfolio apps has to offer but this one, is rather simple and more convenient to use. You don't need to connect any API or any wallet, just put the amount, the buy price and the app will do its algorithm.
